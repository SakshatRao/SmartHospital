About

Healthcare is centered around patients. Everything done by healthcare workers is eventually for the patients' benefit. It therefore goes without saying that patients should be the most important stakeholders in a hospital business.

Healthcare in India is taking massive strides in not only advancing medical technology but also improving patient experience. This is a humble attempt at creating an IoT-based Smart Hospital environment. Such systems utilize the powerful tools of embedded system design and web connectivity to enhance patient experience at hospitals. We hope that such a setup in a hospital can improve its Quality of Service and provide good value for money.
